How PantryBit Works

    

      Enter your available ingredients or use the built-in OCR to scan a photo of your pantry or fridge. PantryBit uses a local recipe database and a scheduling algorithm to build a custom recipe plan. You’ll get a prioritized, parallelized timeline (so you can do multiple things at once), a checklist, and a one-line shopping gap for missing items — all instantly, all in your browser.
    

    
    
      
  • No account or cloud required — everything runs locally for speed and privacy.
    • 
      
  • Great for meal prep, weeknight dinners, or last-minute cooking inspiration.
    • 
      
  • Supports export and print so you can follow your plan your way.
